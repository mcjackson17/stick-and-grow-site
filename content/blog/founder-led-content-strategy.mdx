Your prospects are reading content from 12 different SaaS companies right now. Every one of those companies hired a content agency. Every one of those agencies writes the same recycled takes.

That is why they all sound the same. And that is why none of them are booking demos.

## The Generic Content Trap

Here is the playbook most B2B SaaS companies follow:

1. Hire a content agency or freelancer
2. Hand them a list of SEO keywords
3. Wait for "thought leadership" to appear
4. Wonder why inbound pipeline stays flat

The problem is not the format. The problem is the source. A ghostwriter who has never sat in your customer's chair cannot write content that makes your prospect think "this person gets it."

## Why Founder-Led Content Wins

When a founder writes (or speaks, and someone captures it), three things happen that no agency can replicate:

- **Pattern recognition** -- You have seen hundreds of customer conversations. You know the exact objections, the internal politics, the budget fights. That context bleeds into every sentence.
- **Contrarian credibility** -- You can say "the industry is wrong about X" because you built something that proves it. An agency writer cannot make that claim.
- **Trust velocity** -- Prospects who consume founder content arrive on sales calls pre-sold. They quote your posts back to your AE. The deal cycle shrinks.

<Callout type="tip">
The goal is not to become a "content creator." The goal is to extract 45 minutes of your thinking per week and turn it into a system that feeds your sales team warm leads every Friday.
</Callout>

## How to Start Without Burning Out

You do not need to write 5 posts a week. You need a system:

1. **One 45-minute call per week** -- Talk about what you are seeing in the market, what customers are asking, what you would tell a prospect over coffee.
2. **A team that captures and formats** -- That call becomes 5 social posts, 1 blog, and 1 newsletter. Your voice, your ideas, zero writing time.
3. **A distribution engine** -- Content gets published, tracked, and the engagement data feeds back to your sales team as a warm lead list every Friday.

That is the Stick & Grow model. You talk. We build the pipeline.
